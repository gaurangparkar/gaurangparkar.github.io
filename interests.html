Other Interests

I sometimes play for a local punk-inspired band called tri stk.. I am always looking for new musical projects to indulge in whenever I have the time. Contact me if you have any cool ideas and need a collaboration.

  • Mountain biking: I enjoy riding my bike on mountain trails especially going downhill.
